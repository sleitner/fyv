Motivation

Voter turnout is low in the US. Midterm elections typically draw about 40 percent of the eligible public and in 2014 turnout was only 36.4 percent nationally, down to 28.8% in New York State. Motivation through social networks and social pressure in general has been shown to substantially motivate voting. Share this app and help leverage social pressure to increase civic engagement in our democracy.
