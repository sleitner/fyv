Motivation

Voter turnout is low in the US. Midterm elections typically draw about 40 percent of the eligible public and in 2014 turnout was only 36.4 percent nationally, down to 28.8% in New York . Social pressure has been shown to successfully and dramatically motivate potential voters. Share this app and help leverage social pressure to increase civic engagement in our democracy.
