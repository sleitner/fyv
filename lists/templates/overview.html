Site Usage

Friend your vote is a tool to help mobilize voters by providing easy access to public voting records along with some context, and twitter interaction. Currently, data is only available for New York State.


The site can either be used to look up specific voters, or you can populate a look up table with the people you follow on twitter using the button on the right. The following screen-cast steps through the sites main features.

Voting data

Voter files are recorded by county officials, and then aggregated by secretary of state offices into statewide databases. These databases can be obtained for a small fee in CSV files on CD. The fields for a given record include a voter's name, date of birth, gender, address, party affiliation, registration status, registration date, and an unstructured description of past election participation among other fields.
